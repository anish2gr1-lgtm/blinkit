# Example:
Business Problem - This project was needed because Blinkit did not have a single, consolidated view of its sales data, customer feedback, and outlet performance. Most data was scattered across files, and reporting 
was largely manual. Because of this, making data-driven decisions was slow and inefficient for stakeholders.

Goal of the Dashboard - To help the decision makers easily understand how the business was performing across products and outlets.

Walk through of key visuals 
Key KPIs: Total Sales: $1.20M in, Average Sales: $140.99, Number of Items Sold: 8523, Avg Customer Rating: 3.9 in 2 categories Low Fat and Regular Fat.
Outlet Location Filter Panel: An interactive slicer let users filter all visuals by selecting Tier 1, Tier 2 and Tier 3 locations 
Outlet Size by Donut Chart: Display the distribution of outlet size by high, medium and low sizes. 
Outlet Location by Funnel: Display the distribution of outlet location as Tier 1, Tier2 and Tier 3. 

Business Impact and Insights:
The dashboard also showed a strong relationship between customer ratings and sales performance, where higher-rated products and outlets consistently generated more revenue. With this setup, stakeholders could 
quickly identify top-performing and low-performing products and outlets, reduce manual reporting efforts, and take faster, data-driven decisions related to inventory planning, promotions, and outlet optimization. 
Insights:
1.Sales were highly concentrated in a limited number of product categories and outlet types.
2.Some products had high availability but low sales and poor ratings, which pointed to overstocking and low customer preference.
3.Customer ratings showed a strong relationship with sales performance.
